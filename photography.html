One of my past times is photography. I enjoy travel and have been lucky enough to see some amazing places and sites.

In November 2008 I took 3 months leave from work to travel around South America, after taking some fantastic pictures on a previous trip to Australia using a Sony DSC-P73, I decided to upgrade to a digital SLR.

Being a backpacking holiday I decided on the small and lightweight Nikon D40. I'm very pleased with the results which you can find here.

I host all of my (good) photos on Flickr.
